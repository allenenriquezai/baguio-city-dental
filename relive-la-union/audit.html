2. Only 1 Google review

Finding

Your Google Business Profile shows "Not yet rated (1 Review)." For a skin and laser clinic, that's below the trust floor.

Why this matters

  • Patients considering laser treatment — whitening, hair removal, slimming — are spending real money and want proof it's safe. They check Google reviews before booking.
  • 1 review reads as brand new or unproven. The trust floor for aesthetic clinics is 25+ reviews. Below that, most new patients skip to the next option.
  • You have 5.6K Facebook followers — that's a patient base that trusts you. None of that trust shows on Google where NEW patients are looking.

What your competitors are doing

  • CHARM Medical (San Fernando): Established presence, Google profile with ratings visible
  • B SKINMED (San Fernando): Active on Google Business with reviews
  • The rule: Once you hit 25+ reviews, patients stop second-guessing. A simple SMS or FB message after each visit asking for a Google review gets you there in 3 months.

Fix

Send a short message to every patient after their visit: "Hi! How was your session? If you have 1 minute, your Google review helps us a lot — [link]." FREE to set up. Takes 2 minutes per patient.

3. No online booking

Finding

There is no way for patients to book a session online. Your Facebook page has no "Book Now" button linking to a scheduling system. New patients have to send a message, wait for a reply, and go back and forth to confirm a slot.

Why this matters

  • Patients who decide they want laser whitening at 10pm on a Sunday can't book. They message you, you're closed, they message your competitor, your competitor replies — you lose the booking.
  • After-hours is when people research and decide. B SKINMED has Setmore booking live 24/7. A patient can pick their service, pick a time, and confirm in under 2 minutes.
  • You're paying for followers who want to buy from you, but you're making it hard.

What your competitors are doing

  • B SKINMED (San Fernando): bskinmed.setmore.com — live 24/7 booking, pick service, pick time, done
  • Idara Aesthetics: Online contact form + booking via website
  • Any Setmore/Calendly user: FREE booking page in under 30 minutes

Fix

Set up a FREE Setmore account, add your services and available hours, then put the booking link as your Facebook "Book Now" button. Cost = ₱0. Takes 30 minutes once.

4. No auto-reply or response-time signal

Finding

Your Facebook page shows no "Typically replies within…" response time badge. There is no pinned post or page section showing patients what to expect after they send a message.

Why this matters

  • When a patient sends a DM to your page and sees no response badge, they don't know if they'll wait 5 minutes or 5 hours. Most of them open the next clinic in their search results.
  • Clinics with "Typically replies within minutes" get 30-40% more DM responses converted to bookings — because patients know someone is there.
  • An AI auto-reply handles the initial question ("What are your rates for whitening?") instantly, at any hour, without your staff doing anything.

What your competitors are doing

  • Any clinic with AI auto-reply: Instantly answers rate questions, books consultations, sends the price list — before your team even wakes up
  • Response-time badge: FB grants "Very Responsive to Messages" badge when you reply within 1 hour consistently — it shows on your page and in search results

Fix

Set up a Facebook Page auto-reply for common questions (rates, services, how to book). The setup is free inside Facebook Business Suite. An AI layer on top handles anything more complex.
