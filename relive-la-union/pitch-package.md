## Call-close script (15-min discovery)

**Opener:** "Salamat sa oras ninyo. Nakita niyo yung audit? Tama ba yung gaps na nakita ko?"

**Frame:** "Ang nakita ko sa Relive La Union: walang website, 1 Google review lang, walang online booking. Yung competitors ninyo — B SKINMED, CHARM, Idara — lahat may website na at may 24/7 booking. Pero yung brand ninyo is mas maganda sa kanilang lahat. The infrastructure lang ang kulang."

**Offer:** "Free 30-day pilot. Mag-set up kami ng website, booking, at AI auto-reply. Ikaw ang magde-decide kung tama para sa clinic. Walang bayad, walang commitment. Kung gusto niyo ituloy after 30 days, that's when we talk retainer."

**Retainer pricing:** PHP 25,000-40,000/month depending on scope.

**Close:** "Pwede ko bang i-send yung 1-page scope ng pilot para makita ninyo exactly what gets built?"

## Objection handlers

- _"May staff na kaming nag-re-reply sa Messenger"_ → "Perfect — hindi namin papalitan yung staff ninyo. Yung AI handles after-hours at FAQs para hindi sila ma-overload. Sila pa rin ang mag-decide sa complex questions."
- _"Magkano yung retainer?"_ → "Pilot is FREE. After 30 days, PHP 25-40k/month depende sa scope. At yung website at booking system — yours to keep kahit wala kaming retainer."
- _"Hindi pa kami ready"_ → "Understood. 30-day pilot means you test it first, zero cost. If it works, we continue. If not, you keep everything we built."

## Key angles for Relive La Union specifically

- **Multi-branch opportunity:** Baguio + Cabanatuan + La Union in one hub website is a differentiated play vs all local solo competitors
- **Brand strength:** They already have the strongest brand aesthetic in the market — positioning them as the premium option is easy
- **5.6K followers = untapped list:** A re-engagement sequence to these followers via FB retargeting + DM broadcast would yield immediate bookings
- **Google review gap is easy win:** 1 review → 25+ in 60 days with systematic post-visit ask = instant trust signal
