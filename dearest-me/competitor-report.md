## What the Baguio wellness spa market looks like

Baguio City has a strong wellness market driven by:
- Local residents seeking regular massage and self-care
- Tourists from Metro Manila on weekend trips to Baguio
- BPO and office workers based in Baguio City

The clients who book online (vs. walk-in) are the higher-value segment — they plan ahead, they show up, and they re-book. Those clients go to spas with websites and booking systems.

## The gap

You have 73 Google reviews — more than many of your competitors. That's a real asset. The problem is that your 3.9★ rating and lack of booking infrastructure means clients who find you on Google don't convert. They see the rating, see no website or booking link, and go to the next option.

The fix is infrastructure, not marketing spend. A website + booking link + auto-reply is a one-time setup that runs permanently.
